About Us

“We’re not another brand you have to settle for. We are committed to creating exceptional products that inspire musicians and push the limits of what’s possible. To us, every detail matters — from the quality of the materials to the craftsmanship and collaboration that goes into every design.”

A vision realized

The Birth of Genuwind Customs

My journey into the world of music products began at the tender age of 14. Driven by a profound, youthful passion for music and a growing frustration with the industry’s stagnation in innovation and quality, I took destiny into my own hands. I began creating my own music accessories, determined to make a difference. By the time I was 17, I was elated to see professional musicians embracing the products I crafted. It was more than gratifying; it was a powerful affirmation that the world was hungry for high-quality music accessories.

From Frustration to Innovation

As I trained to become a professional musician, the glaring lack of quality and innovation in available music products struck me deeply. This revelation fueled my determination to establish Genuwind Customs—a company born from a commitment to excellence. I knew that musicians, above all, understood the nuances of what made a superior music accessory. Therefore, Genuwind Customs is proudly owned and operated by musicians, ensuring every product is crafted with unmatched precision.

Innovators First, Manufacturers Second

Process

At Genuwind Customs, we see ourselves as innovators first and manufacturers second. Our dedication to innovation led us to creating our research division in 2022. Our new slogan, “Where Research Meets Rhythm,” encapsulates our ethos. As a musician, your focus should be on honing your craft and reaching new heights, not worrying about the reliability of your accessories. We take immense pride in our rigorous design process—from initial concept to final packaging—ensuring our products meet the exacting standards of musicians like you.

Our ultimate goal is to set new benchmarks for music accessories within the industry. Our Team

Shivam patel

founder, lead engineer

“As a saxophonist, I’m constantly amazed by technological developments in the engineering fields. I always try to find ways to incorporate them in our art though our products, because at the end of the day if musicians like us don’t innovate, no one will.”

Starting his first musical-product designs at 14 years old, Shivam has an itch for developing highly innovative products. He has developed four patents and numerous technologies under Genuwind Customs. He has over nine different engineering certifications from industry leaders and is a PMI certified engineer. Shivam hopes to create easier lives for everyone from young students to retired soloists.

Having been classically trained on the saxophone at NYU, Shivam is also in touch with the freelancing side of music. He first made his debut with the Gary Civic Orchestra, playing Villa-Lobos’ concerto for soprano saxophone under former NBC musician Mike Carson. He has played in various musical scenarios, such as session recordings, jazz bands, pit orchestras, symphonic orchestras, and chamber ensembles.
