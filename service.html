Mouthpiece replication & Repair

Professional musician who needs an exact replica? Hobbyist who just broke his vintage 1940’s mouthpiece? Whoever you are, we can help you replicate your mouthpiece.

micron-accurate rePLICAS

To put it in perspective, a human hair is typically between 70 to 100 microns thick. So, when we talk about micron accuracy, we’re dealing with extremely fine measurements.

DON’T SETTLE

Many mouthpiece specialists can laser scan a mouthpiece, but in reality, no laser can accurately scan the inside of mouthpieces; believe us, we’ve tried. The best scanners can do is estimate the remaining topography of the mouthpiece and then have a person or program estimate the remaining model. However, we know that any small change to the model will cause a drastic sound change. To combat this, we developed our own process to ensure you will get a 1:1 mirror replica of your original mouthpiece. The best part? We store the 3D models for you. So if you ever want to reorder, you can get as many replicas as you want, as fast as in 2 days.
